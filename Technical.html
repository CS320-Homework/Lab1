Technical Background


  I enrolled in CS3220 because it is an upper division requirement. Despite the requirement, I think it is important to learn about front end development. The more I become familiar with different languages the more of an asset I will be to a potential employer.


  I expect to learn the fundamentals of building a website in this course. I also expect to learn how the front end and the back end work together. Where does the data go once you click "Submit"? Learning how websites secure customer's information would be great too. Hopefully, through practice, I will be able to build a website on the spot, if asked by a friend, professor, or employer. By understanding how front and back end development work I will have a better idea of which field I want to specialize in.
